# 📊 Customer Churn Prediction using Machine Learning

## 💼 Business Problem
Companies lose millions due to customer churn. This project predicts high-risk customers using Machine Learning and provides actionable business insights for customer retention.

---

## 🛠️ Technologies & Tech Stack
* **Language:** Python
* **Libraries:** Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-Learn, Matplotlib, Seaborn
* **Model:** Random Forest Classifier
* **Performance:** **95.06% Accuracy**

## 💡 Business Insights & Data Findings
* **Tenure is King:** Customer tenure is the single strongest predictor of churn. Newer customers are at a much higher risk.
* **Cashback Impact:** Cashback offers significantly affect retention rates.
* **Logistics Matter:** Customers living farther from fulfillment warehouses display a noticeably higher churn rate.

---

## 🎯 Strategic Business Recommendations
1. **Enhance Onboarding:** Design targeted engagement programs for low-tenure customers during their first 3 months to build loyalty.
2. **Optimize Cashback Incentives:** Restructure the cashback and rewards policy to incentivize long-term retention rather than one-time purchases.
3. **Resolve Delivery Distance Pain Points:** Investigate delivery delays or higher shipping costs for regions located far from warehouses and offer localized promotional discounts or premium shipping tier trials.
